Aim

Convert Waste Materials for Welfare of Stray Animals


Newspapers

The main aim of C2 is to raise fund for stray animals by collecting old newspaper, magazines etc.

Plastic Waste

C2 can also raise fund for stray animals by collecting old plastic bottles etc.

Other Items

Animal lovers may also contribute by other means like Cash, Medicines, Dog Food, Bread, Milk, Biscuits etc.

About us

C2 is providing a social platform to contribute for welfare of stray voiceless souls. C2 will collect these items from your homes/offices on monthly basis. Accordingly funds will be generated by selling these old items and will be utilised on need basis for welfare of stray voiceless souls.
